Overdose deaths in Connecticut

This website is dedicated to providing in-depth information on overdose deaths in Connecticut from 2018-2022. Additional years will be added when available. All data is from the Connecticut Office of the Chief Medical Examiner and comprises all decedents with an accidental/undetermined cause of death where opioids were detected in the postmortem toxicology report.
